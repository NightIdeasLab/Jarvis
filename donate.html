Donating for Jarvis

Why Donate to Jarvis?

Jarvis is free, but costs us money and time to produce, support, and distribute. This gift to the developers would demonstrate your appreciation of this software and help its future development.

If you'd like to make a one-time donation to Night Ideas Lab Inc., you can use PayPal to make it fast and easy.

PayPal members can donate directly from their PayPal accounts by logging in via the PayPal website and clicking "send money" to nightideaslab@gmail.com, or just click the button below.
Non-PayPal members can still pay with a credit card without signing up for PayPal. Click the button below then follow the instructions at the bottom of the page for non-PayPal members.
Either way, it's a convenient way to support Night Ideas Lab Inc. and Jarvis.
