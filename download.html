App Update

This means that you already have the app installed on your computer.

Open the application, then click on the "Jarvis" menu item in the menu bar. In the menu you will see "Check for Update..." click on it and the app should update itself it an update exists.

After the process is done it will notify you if there exist an update or you have the latest version.

If for any reasons you receive an error to the update process please contact us.
